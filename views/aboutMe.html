POS
The point of sale (POS), an important part of the point of purchase, is the place where customers can pay for goods or services and pay sales taxes. These can be physical stores, where terminals and POS systems are used to process card payments, or virtual points of sale such as computers or mobile electronic devices.

How does our POS system work?

We provide small business a jumpstart, where you can manage your money and sales orders professionally and securely.

We provide affordable cash registers for you to maintain and are suitable for any type of business you have. Our cash register is a web based application, where you can make your iPad or computer as your cash register.

You can also request a bundle from us, if you prefer to buy a full set of cash registers.

Your data is secure in the cloud and only you and your employees can have access to the sales orders you have acquired.

Our cashiers can also store your inventory data. It's as simple as, you set the inventory amount for each item you want to sell on the first day using our system, then our cash register will count the number of items you sell and tell you to restock.
